# Brain Dump Terms of Service Draft

Status: draft for product planning. Review with counsel before public launch.

## Service

Brain Dump helps users organize free-form text into tasks, calendar events, projects, and waiting-on reminders.

## Beta Status

During beta, Brain Dump is experimental. Features may change, fail, or be removed. Users should review created tasks and calendar events for accuracy.

## User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

- The content they submit.
- Reviewing created tasks and calendar events.
- Keeping their Google account secure.
- Disconnecting Brain Dump if they no longer want it connected.

## Google Account Access

Brain Dump connects to Google only after the user grants permission. Brain Dump uses that access to provide requested task and calendar functionality. Users can revoke access through Brain Dump or their Google Account settings.

## No Automatic Email Sending

Brain Dump does not send email during beta. Email-like requests should be captured for review instead of being sent automatically.

## Acceptable Use

Users may not use Brain Dump to:

- Violate laws or third-party rights.
- Submit malicious, abusive, or unlawful content.
- Interfere with the service.
- Attempt unauthorized access to other accounts or data.

## Availability

Brain Dump is provided as available. The service may be interrupted for maintenance, provider issues, or beta changes.

## Accuracy

Brain Dump may parse or route items incorrectly. Users should verify important calendar events, deadlines, tasks, and follow-ups.

## Limitation of Liability

Draft legal language needed before public launch.

## Termination

Brain Dump may suspend or remove access for misuse, security risk, or beta program changes.
