# Brain Dump Launch Announcement Kit

Use these drafts when opening a beta cohort or announcing the public beta seed. Keep the promise narrow and review the
current `/status`, `/roadmap`, `/pricing`, and `/security` pages before publishing.

## One-Sentence Description

Brain Dump turns messy thoughts into reviewed Google Tasks, Calendar events, projects, and follow-ups.

## Short Public Post

Brain Dump is opening a small beta for people who live in Google Tasks or Calendar and need a faster way to turn messy
thoughts into a reviewed plan.

It does not send email, make unreviewed Google changes, or charge during beta. You stay in control before anything is
created.

Start here: `[BRAIN_DUMP_FRONTEND_ORIGIN]/beta`

## Longer Public Post

I am building Brain Dump for the moment when everything is in your head at once: tasks, errands, meetings, follow-ups,
projects, and half-formed reminders.

The beta version lets you paste or dictate the mess, review the organized output, and then create approved items in your
own Google Tasks and Calendar.

The current beta is intentionally limited:

- No email sending.
- No unreviewed Google changes.
- No paid plan yet.
- No team workspace yet.
